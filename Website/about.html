ABOUT US AND OUR FANTASTIC
GAME

Parabellum was inspired by our favorite action movie.
Some strange things have been going on in the world lately. We need your insticts and your courage to save it.
You should challenge yourself and prove us that we can change this reality.
Parabellum is a difficult world to live in...

Use your fighting skills to win or die as a hero!
